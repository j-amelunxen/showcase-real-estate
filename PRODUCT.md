# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Two audiences. The fictional one: ultra-high-net-worth investors browsing on large screens, in no hurry, expecting to be courted rather than sold to. The real one: prospective clients of the site's author evaluating this page as a portfolio showcase — the page must demonstrate front-end craft (scroll-driven motion, cinematic pacing, restraint under pressure).

## Product Purpose

A fictional luxury real-estate landing page ("Immo-Showcase") that exists to showcase premium web design and engineering. Success = a visitor thinks "this feels like a €10M brokerage" and "whoever built this knows what they're doing." All contact details and properties are fictional; only /impressum is real.

## Brand Personality

Cinematic, discreet, assured. The tone of an old private bank that happens to sell villas: it never raises its voice, it lets imagery and typography do the persuading. Emotions to evoke: quiet awe, trust, exclusivity.

## Anti-references

- Generic SaaS landing pages (gradient blobs, glassmorphism, hero-metric cards).
- Loud real-estate portals (Immoscout, Zillow): filters, badges, urgency banners.
- Crypto/"luxury lifestyle" flash: gold-on-black excess, particle effects, bounce animations.

## Design Principles

- **Cinema, not decoration.** Motion tells the story (scroll-driven reveals, parallax depth, counting numbers); nothing moves without narrative purpose.
- **Deep-teal stage, mint spotlight.** Ink (#14323a) and warm stone carry the surface; the mint accent (#00ffbb) marks attention on dark surfaces, deep teal (#0f766e) stands in on light ones. (Rebranded 2026-07 from black/amber, inspired by gocanopy.tech's design system.)
- **Typography is the luxury.** Haboro Norm serif in uppercase for statements, light Geist for supporting text; scale contrast does the hierarchy work.
- **Everything wired, nothing fake.** Buttons and nav scroll to real anchors; no dead links (see CLAUDE.md conventions).
- **Respect the viewer.** Reduced-motion users get a calm, static page, not a broken one.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

No formal WCAG target, but: honor `prefers-reduced-motion`, keep text contrast high (white on black, near-black on white), all videos are decorative (`aria-hidden`, muted), keyboard-reachable controls.
